Music Software

Ableton Live

A powerful DAW known for live performance and loop-based music creation.

GarageBand

Apple's easy-to-use DAW perfect for beginners and mobile creators.

Soundtrap

A collaborative online DAW ideal for remote music making and podcasts.

Rating Scale

  • 1. Ableton is most well-known, especially in professional music production and live performance.
  • 2. GarageBand - Medium well-known, popular among beginners and casual music creators, especially on Apple devices.
  • 3. Soundtrap - Not as well-known as the other two, though gaining popularity for its online collaboration features.
